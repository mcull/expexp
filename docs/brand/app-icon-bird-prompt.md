# Expexp — Bird App-Icon Prompt (for an image model)

Paste into an image generator (Claude design, Midjourney, DALL·E, etc.). The key is that it reads
as **one bird moving across a static sky** (a fading motion trail), not a flock.

## Prompt

> A square iOS **app icon**, full-bleed, no text, no rounded corners. A **single bird captured as a
> film multiple-exposure**: one graceful swift/swallow in flight crossing **left to right**, shown
> as **3–4 overlapping ghosted positions** that fade behind a crisp leading bird — a clear motion
> trail of the *same* bird, not a flock. The bird soars across a **large, soft, glowing sun** set
> low in the frame (the static backdrop). **Cinematic, filmic mood**: deep teal-blue sky up top
> blending into warm amber and maroon below, golden-hour light, gentle **film grain and halation
> glow** around the sun and the bird's edges; the areas where the bird's ghosts overlap are slightly
> **denser/darker**, like real stacked exposures. Painterly and atmospheric, **analog film
> aesthetic**. Bold, simple, balanced, centered — legible even at tiny sizes. 1:1.

## Tips
- If it makes a flock, add: *"one single bird repeated in a fading left-to-right motion trail,
  evenly spaced, same pose, decreasing opacity."*
- If too busy/detailed, add: *"minimal, few elements, strong silhouette."*
- Ask for a few variants: **sun vs. crescent moon**, **warmer vs. cooler**, **bird larger vs. smaller**.

## Output specs to enforce
1024×1024 PNG, square, full-bleed, **no transparency**, **no pre-rounded corners**, **no text**.
Keep key content within the central ~80%. Drop the winner in and we'll wire it into the asset catalog.
