## Description (≤4000)
Expexp brings the analog magic of double exposure to your iPhone — stacking several frames into one
luminous, filmic image.

Take a shot, then another. Expexp blends them the way film does: an equal-weight, linear-light
average finished with a gentle film tone curve. Every exposure counts the same (no frame gets
buried), highlights glow, and you never get the muddy, false-color artifacts of crude digital
blends.

Two ways to anchor a stack:
• Edge Lock (rear camera) — freezes the static scene, like a building or a street, while traffic
and people smear into a soft, ghostly cloud.
• Face Lock (front camera) — pins your face while the world swirls around you as you move.

A live gyro "level" reticle helps you settle back onto your first frame — a calm, analog feedback
loop, not digital trickery. A translucent ghost of your previous shots shows through so you can line
up the next one. Shoot in portrait or landscape, and choose a look: Neutral, Filmic, or Moody.

No accounts. No clutter. No data collection. Just light, layered.
