# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to **Expexp** and other apps published by **Cull Ventures, LLC**
("we," "us"). We built these apps to respect your privacy completely.

## The short version
**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no
analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no servers.** Everything the app does happens on your
device.

## Camera and Photos
Expexp is a camera app, so it asks for two permissions:

- **Camera** — used only to let you take photos inside the app. The live camera feed is never
  recorded, uploaded, or sent anywhere.
- **Photo Library (Add)** — used only to save the photos *you* create into your own Photo Library
  when you tap Save.

All image processing (aligning, blending, and creating your double exposures) happens **locally on
your device**. Your photos never leave your device except when you choose to save them to your own
Photo Library, which stays under your control.

## Data we collect
**None.** We do not collect names, emails, location, contacts, usage analytics, device identifiers,
or any other personal information. Because we collect nothing, we have nothing to sell, share, or
lose.

## Third parties
The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with
third parties.

## Children
Because the app collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages.

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.
